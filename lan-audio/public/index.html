Same Wi-Fi Audio Relay

Send your PC sound to your phone v2.

Open the host page on the PC in Chrome or Edge, route audio into VB-CABLE, and then open the listener page on your phone.

Open PC Host Open Phone Listener

How it works

1. Run npm run lan-audio on this PC.

2. On the PC, open http://localhost:43117/host.

3. Route the app audio you want into CABLE Input, then open one of the listener URLs shown below on the phone.
